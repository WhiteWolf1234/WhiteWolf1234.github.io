Legend is a great book that I have just finished reading. Legend is the first young adult book in the trilogy by Marie Lu. The second book is Prodigy and the third is Champion. The Legend trilogy takes place in the future. Legend is a amazing, suspensful book that I think you would love.
The Neptune Project is a amazing book that when you start read you can't let it go. The author is Polly Holyoke. The book is about a girl who has to live under water for the rest of her life. She was to survive the dangers of the underwater world.
